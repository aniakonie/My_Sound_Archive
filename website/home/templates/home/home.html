Music arranged into genre folders

Easier to search through your artists when they are divided into genres. Genre classification is fluid so you can always move any artist to another folder if the one assigned by the app does not suit your preferences.

Separate folder for each artist

Each artist folder comprises tracks sourced from your list of liked songs and playlists. No need to worry if you've overlooked liking certain songs by your favorite artists that are exclusively present in your playlists.



Every song matters

A dedicated folder for each genre housing tracks from artists with a limited presence in your library. Artist folders exclusively feature your most-listened-to musicians, while the 'loose tracks' section gathers those unique finds which are equally important.

Artist's remixes and featured tracks included

Enjoying an artist with an extensive remix collection? Despite remix authors not being the main artists of these tracks, they are still included in the artist folder. Featured tracks and collaborations are showcased as well.

Please note that this website is still under development.



My Sound Archive utilizes the Spotify API to retrieve data for users. It is currently in development mode,
which means that it can serve up to 25 users (according to Spotify's rules).

If you wish to create your sound archive, please let us know by sending an email to the following address: mysoundarchiveofficial@gmail.com
We need to grant you permission first.

In the near future, we plan to:


- submit an extension request to Spotify for the app (to serve more users),
- address library retrieval failures caused by temporary issues in Spotify's backend,
- improve the algorithm for assigning genres to artists,
- add an option to modify genres assigned by the app,
- integrate Google authentication.
