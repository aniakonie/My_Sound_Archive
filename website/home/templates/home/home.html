Welcome to Virtual Music Library



All your favorite tracks in one place.

Keep all of your music neatly organized so that you have a bird's-eye view of the collection.



Currently available for users only



Music arranged into genre folders
Easier to search through your artists when they are divided into genres. Genre classification is fluid so you can always move any artist to another folder if the one assigned by the app does not suit you.
Separate folder for each artist
Each artist folder comprises tracks sourced from your list of liked songs and playlists. No need to worry if you've overlooked liking certain songs by your favorite artists that are exclusively present in your playlists.


Every song matters
A dedicated folder for each genre housing tracks from artists with a limited presence in your library. Artist folders exclusively feature your most-listened-to musicians, while the 'loose tracks' section gathers those unique finds which are equally important.
Artist's remixes and featured tracks included
Enjoying an artist with an extensive remix collection? Despite remix authors not being the main artists, they are still included in the artist folder. Featured tracks are showcased as well.
